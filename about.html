What We Believe

Humanity
Christianity teaches that all people are born into sin and no one is good enough to earn salvation. It's this sin that separates mankind from God with whom they can only.
God in The Person
It is believed that there are beings, created by God, that influence mankind. Angels strive to further God's purpose and message and demons' goal is to lead people away.
Salvation
Specific beliefs vary from denomination to denomination, even person to person, but core values and principles often remain the same from one church to another.
Grace
Specific beliefs vary from denomination to denomination, even person to person, but core values and principles often remain the same from one church to another.
  • 1

    Christianity is founded on the belief that there is one God composed of three distinct persons: God the Father, Jesus Christ his Son, and the Holy Spirit.
  • 2

    Christians believe God created the world in six days, resting on the seventh. They believe man is His most prized creation.
  • 3

    Jesus Christ is God's Son and the only way to salvation and forgiveness of sins. Christians believe he was sent to earth, born from a virgin, was crucified.
  • 4

    Christians' holy book, the Bible, is believed to be God's inspired word, a historical document, and a guide for faithful living.
Life Group
